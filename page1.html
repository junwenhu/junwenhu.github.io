My Story

EDUCATION
I'm now a doctoral student and a graduate teaching assistant in Communication at Michigan State University. I earned a BA and MA in the discipline at Nanjing University, China.

WHO AM I
I'm a first-generation college student who came from a small motor town in China to another motor town('s neighboring city). My childhood idol Madonna was also born in Michigan. So hey it seems the dream came true. You must be my lucky star!

Growing up in the working class community where I was surrounded by all the cool big locomotives and gradually developed my interests in mechanics and technical stuff. Therefore, it came naturally that I largely picked up some quantitative analysis and computational skills by myself because I think it much fun to play with all the technical stuff!
