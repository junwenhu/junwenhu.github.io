I am a Ph.D. student at the Department of Communication, Michigan State University. I study the processes of people seeking relationships in high-choice social and media enviroments, from online mate-searching to taking care of the psychological distress from bad relationship-seeking experience. With such a focus, my research topics encompass online dating, social network, social support, algorithm, artificial intelligence (AI), and misinformation.
