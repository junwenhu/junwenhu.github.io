I am a Ph.D. student at the Department of Communication, Michigan State University. My research is situated in the intersection of interpersonal communication and human-AI communication. One focus has been interpersonal AI, for example, layperson beliefs about recommendation systems in online dating and human-chatbot interaction. Another focus is on social support messages and networking behaviors.
