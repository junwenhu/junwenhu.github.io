I am a Ph.D. candidate (all-but-dissertation) at the Department of Communication, Michigan State University. My research focuses on promoting emotional and social well-being through supportive communication strategies, which involve messages, networks, and technologies. My work has been reported by scientific media like PsyPost and Phys.org. I am also a top student paper winner at our top conference (ICA) and have published in high-impact journals such as Computers in Human Behavior (IF = 9.0) as the sole or first author.
